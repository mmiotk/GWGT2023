ACCOMODATION

The participants must book his/her own accommodation. It is possible to book a place in a university dormitory. The residence is located in Sopot approximately 30 minutes on foot or 15 minutes by public transport from Hotel Eureka. The price for a double room is about PLN 80. You can reserve places by contacting the organizers by e-mail at gwgt@ug.edu.pl. Note: the number of places is limited, the order of applications will be taken into account.

10th Slovenian conference on Graph Theory (SICGT)


Overview

IMPORTANT DATES

In order to meet the expectations of people who cannot take part in a stationary meeting, we have decided that GWGT will be a conference in hybrid format.


Important dates (for in person participation)
Early (extended) registration (including payment and reduced fee):31 March, 2023, 2023 (AoE)
Registration deadline (including payment):22 May, 2023 (AoE)
Title/Abstract submission deadline:30 May, 2023 (AoE)
Workshop:June 26-30, 2023
   
Important dates (for on-line participation)
Registration with contributed talk (including payment):22 May, 2023 (AoE)
Registration without contributed talk (including payment):22 May, 2023 (AoE)
Title/Abstract submission deadline:30 May, 2023 (AoE)
Workshop:June 26-30, 2023
Note: the registration is complete only after the conference fee has been paid.

The workshop program will consist of five invited talks, a number of contributed talks (20 minutes) during the five days of sessions and poster session. In addition, three tutorials (3-4h) will be conducted. We will begin on Monday afternoon (about 2 pm) and finish on Friday early afternoon.

Please direct all feedback to the organizing committee chairs.
